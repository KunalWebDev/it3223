LONDON THE SMALLEST CITY IN ENGLAND!

That's right, The megacity the most of us know as Lodon is actually called 'Greater London', whilst the City of London itself is a small portion of this sprawling metropolis. It has a population of only 9,123 people, making it the smallest in England

TECHNICALLY A FOREST CITY

We've spoken before about how green the capital is. It's hard to grasp unless you've visited , but the amount of greenery you can enjoy in London is enormous. So much so, that the desnity of the trees it has per square mile qualifies it as a forest as per the United Nations definition

'BIG BEN' AIN'T REALLY BIG BEN

Tourists from all over come to stand on Westminister Bridge and take a selfie next to Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament. Unfortunately, not one of them ever leaves with a picture of it. This world-famous landmark actually named the Elizabeth Tower - Big Ben is the name of the bell that lives inside. So you won't snap a photo of Big Ben but if you're lucky you might get to hear it

LONDON AIN'T THAT RAINY

Rumor says that London is quite rainy but the opposite is actaully true. The capital may be known for being a rainy city except for the fact, that its NOT TRUE AT ALL! London actaully gets less rain than Rome, Maimi, and Syndey both in terms of volume and number of rainy days.
